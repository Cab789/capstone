If you are a research scholar and your work requires access to the full text of more than 500 cases per day, you may apply for unrestricted access to the full text of all cases made available by the Caselaw Access Project.

To be eligible, you must demonstrate that you are a research scholar and you must enter into a special bulk access agreement with LexisNexis, which prohibits you from (1) using the cases for non-research or commercial purposes and (2) sharing bulk caselaw with others.

We offer several ways to obtain unrestricted access:

Harvard community members

If you are a member of the Harvard community with a Harvard email address, you can sign the Harvard bulk access agreement and you will immediately have full access from Harvard IP addresses.

Other research scholars

Sign this application to request access and your application will be reviewed shortly.

If you would like to explore commercial use of unrestricted data or have other questions, please contact us.
