Eligibility for Unmetered Access to CAP Cases

To be eligible for unmetered access to CAP cases, you must be:

Restrictions on Data Usage and Distribution

The CAP cases are being made available by the Library pursuant to its agreement with LexisNexis, a division of RELX Inc., which has certain exclusive rights to make use of the cases for purposes other than education and research. As such, the CAP cases are subject to the following important restrictions on usage and distribution, all of which you must accept in order to receive unmetered access to them:

  • You may use the CAP cases only in connection with educational or research purposes.
  • You may not use the CAP cases in connection with any other purposes, including any commercial purposes.
  • You may not publish, disseminate, distribute or share the CAP cases, except:
    • In furtherance of your educational or research purposes, you may publish, distribute, disseminate or share no more than 500 cases per day;
    • In furtherance of your educational or research purposes, you may publish, distribute, disseminate or share the CAP cases with other researchers who are eligible for comprehensive unmetered access to CAP cases and who have signed this agreement.

By accepting this agreement, you accept these restrictions. If you do not accept these restrictions, then you cannot receive comprehensive unmetered access to CAP cases.
